# HR Policy — NovaTech Solutions

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- 26 days per year (full-time employees)
- Accrual: 2.17 days per month
- Unused days carry over — maximum 10 days to the next calendar year
- Request via HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for absences over 3 days

### Public Holidays
- Poland: all statutory public holidays (approx. 13 per year)
- Germany (Berlin office): all statutory Brandenburg/Berlin public holidays
- Remote employees follow the public holidays of their country of residence

### Sick Leave
- Full pay for the first 33 days per calendar year (Polish Labour Code)
- From day 34: ZUS sickness benefit (80% of salary base)
- Submit sick leave documentation (L4) to hr@novatech.io within 2 working days

### Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: as per Polish Labour Code (up to 52 weeks)
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks fully paid (company top-up)
- Parental leave: up to 32 weeks (as per Labour Code); NovaTech top-up for first 12 weeks

### Unpaid Leave
- Available upon request, subject to team lead and HR approval
- Minimum notice: 4 weeks

## Benefits

### Standard Package (all employees)
- Private medical insurance — LuxMed Gold plan (covers employee + 1 dependent)
- Multisport card — category M (gym, swimming pools, fitness classes)
- Psychological support — 6 free sessions per year via Mindgram platform
- Home office allowance — 100 PLN/month net for remote employees

### Additional Perks
- Lunch card — 400 PLN/month loaded on Edenred card (Warsaw and Kraków offices)
- Training budget — 3 000 PLN/year per employee for courses, books, conferences
- English classes — 2 × 50 min/week, online, during working hours
- Team integration budget — 500 PLN/person per quarter for team events

## Salary & Compensation

- Salary reviews: annually in January, based on performance review and market data
- Performance bonuses: up to 15% of annual salary, paid in Q1 for previous year results
- Referral bonus: 5 000 PLN gross for a successful hire who passes probation

## Performance Reviews

- Frequency: twice a year (June and December)
- Format: self-assessment + team lead assessment + calibration session
- Output: performance rating (Below / Meets / Exceeds Expectations), development plan,
  salary adjustment decision

## Code of Conduct

- Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will result in immediate disciplinary action.
- Conflicts of interest must be declared to HR in writing.
- Confidential company information must not be shared outside NovaTech without written approval.
- All employees must complete the annual GDPR and Information Security training (mandatory).

## Termination

- Notice period: 1 month during probation, 3 months after probation (for employment contracts)
- Garden leave: the company may place employees on garden leave during the notice period
- Offboarding checklist: return equipment, revoke access, knowledge handover session
